THE CONQUERER

The illustration conveys the life of a student, mainly a Montalbeño student. The image, either-or painted canvas, depicts most students' struggles and circumstances, just like the following;

  • High fare cost
  • Traffic
  • Air pollution, land pollution, water pollution, etc.

These circumstances are due to the modifications that occur in our generation; some say that it is an unfathomable and inevitable change that we must bare. Furthermore, the illustration depicts that we must be resilient because life is full of ups and downs; we must be capable of withstanding unforeseen challenges. As trials and challenges continue to oppugn us, we must have the courage and the fortitude to keep moving forward and to transform those into opportunities. Lastly, always keeping our feet on the ground is also a golden rule - being humble, in simple terms. Despite different social statuses, all social classes must be appreciated, respected, and treated fairly; we are just surviving and dealing with the things only we can comprehend the most.
